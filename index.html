Overview of the Company

Anwar General Services Establishment, a major source of human factors in Bangladesh, can help you anticipate and benefit from the changes happening everyday in the contemporary international employment market. We have been actively involved in the recruiting business for last two decades having served our global clients with high degree of excellence especially in Middle Eastern and African countries. The Chairman and CEO of the company Mr. Nurul Anwar Chowdhury is a prominent and reputed recruiting agent in the middle eastern markets since two decades. His dynamic leadership and proactive management outlook heightened the company in an unparalleled benchmark in overseas employment. Anwar General Services Est. has been putting its ceaseless efforts in picking up the best candidates for its clients.
